About Us

Python Club is a community of Python Programmers and Enthusiasts.We work, joke , have fun and generally do amazing things with Python

The club was started as a result of the need for an organized team of computer programmers that stand out from the bunch of other groups.

What makes us stand out is our focus on building things rather than endless number of tutorials.

We have worked together on a lot of different projects which you can check out here.

We're headed by Olamilekan Wahab .

While we try very much to be a body, our activities are regulated by the PYUNG which stands for the Python Users Group in Nigeria
